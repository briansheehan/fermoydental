The Practice

We are four independent General Dental Surgeons operating together in a shared premises in Fermoy Town Centre.

Our independence is an assurance to patients; each of us must stand over our own work.

Sharing our premises, staff and equipment allows us to pass on savings to our clients, as well as giving us dentists the chance to consult with each other informally on a day to day basis.

The practice was established by Charles P. O'Meara B.D.S in 1928 and he was joined by his son David O'Meara B.D.S. in 1963. Andrew O'Brien B.D.S. joined the practice in 1977 as an independent Dental Surgeon. John Mills B.D.S. M.Clin.Dent joined in 2000, also on an independent basis and most recently Cian O'Meara B.D.S. (son of David) joined in 2007.

The practice has established a reputation both locally and nationally in the area of Functional Jaw Orthopedics, with David an Andrew specialising in this treatment for many years. {% endcapture %} {% capture the_team %}
